About Us

The WIRS Lab is a dynamic research group led by Dr. Sayantan (Monty) Majumdar, an Assistant Research Professor at the Desert Research Institute. His work focuses on the intersection of hydrology, data science, and remote sensing.

Dr. Majumdar's background includes experience as a Research Scientist Intern at Facebook and an Analytics Modeling Intern at Planet Labs. Our mission is to explore and solve complex scientific problems through interdisciplinary collaboration and state-of-the-art technology.
